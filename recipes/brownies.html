Rich Chocolate Brownies

A classic, decadent dessert

Dense and fudgy chocolate brownies with a deep cocoa flavour and a crackly top.

Ingredients

  • 225g unsalted butter, melted
  • 250g caster sugar
  • 150g light brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 125g plain flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 175g dark chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (fan 160°C) and line a 23cm x 23cm (9x9 inch) baking tin with baking paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together melted butter, caster sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, whisking until combined.
  4. Sift in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder, and fold gently until just mixed.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, then pour batter into the prepared baking tin.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out with moist crumbs.
  7. Let cool before slicing into squares and serving.
