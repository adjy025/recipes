Eggs Benedict

A breakfast classic

A classic brunch dish featuring poached eggs and rich hollandaise sauce over a toasted English muffin with ham.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 English muffins, halved and toasted
  • 4 slices of British back bacon or ham
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 115g unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Â½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and cayenne pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a heatproof bowl, whisk egg yolks, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and whisk while slowly drizzling in melted butter until thickened. Remove from heat and add cayenne pepper.
  3. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and cook the bacon until lightly browned, then set aside.
  4. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer, add vinegar, and swirl the water. Carefully crack an egg into the water and poach for 3 minutes. Repeat for all eggs.
  5. Place toasted English muffins on plates, top each with a slice of bacon, then a poached egg, and drizzle with hollandaise sauce. Garnish with chives and serve immediately.
